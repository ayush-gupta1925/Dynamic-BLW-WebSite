About BLW Varanasi

Banaras Locomotive Works (BLW), formerly known as Diesel Locomotive Works (DLW), was established in 1961 in Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh. It is one of the premier production units of Indian Railways.

BLW now manufactures high-power electric locomotives like WAP-7 and WAG-9, which are vital for freight and passenger transport across India.

With world-class manufacturing standards and ISO certifications, BLW has earned national awards for its excellence in productivity, quality, and energy conservation.

Powered by advanced technology and a skilled workforce, BLW is a proud contributor to the Make in India initiative by delivering indigenous locomotives for both domestic and international needs.
